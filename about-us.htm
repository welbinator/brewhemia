About Us

Brewhemia is a family owned café located in the historic ZCBJ Hall in New Bohemia District of Cedar Rapids, Iowa. Brewhemia is a name that encompasses a focus on quality beverage while embracing the community and neighborhood of New Bohemia.

At Brewhemia you will find an intimate café featuring amazing coffee, breakfast and lunch, plus a full bar featuring a variety of local craft beers, wine and spirits.
